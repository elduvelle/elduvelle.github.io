Their main points:
- Most studies finding non-spatial responses (e.g. odours, objects) in HPC did not test if these were conjunctive "feature-in-space".
- When tested, most of the responses were indeed location-dependent; very few "feature-only" cells found.

-a "current goal" cell would be active at the preferred goal, this was not found either.
