Reminder to do
rng(‘shuffle’)
before random number generation in Matlab!

Otherwise the random ‘seed’ will always be the same at restart and so will your sequence of ‘random’ numbers.

https://www.mathworks.com/help/matlab/ref/rng.html
(Except if you do parallel processing ) https://twitter.com/TomRhysMarshall/status/1508747937119191043
