So what does SI do?
https://proceedings.neurips.cc/paper/1992/file/5dd9db5e033da9c6fb5ba83c7a7ebea9-Paper.pdf
If a cell fires:
in 1 bin: SI will be high
everywhere: it will be low
1/2 area: in between

Here, spikes are shifted until SI is maximal: isn't this going to shift them to the smallest available region (ie where paths overlap)?
