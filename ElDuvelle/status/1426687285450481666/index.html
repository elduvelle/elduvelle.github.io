3 - Place field repetition - 2016

where we learn that the spatial activity of hippocampal #PlaceCells, which tends to repeat in a parallel maze, stop repeating in an angled one... and that rats are lost in the former but not the latter!

https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/hipo.22496

4 - Splitter cells - 2016

Some place cells can be #SplitterCells, ie, change their firing depending on the goal. Wait. The goal, or the trajectory?

Roddy elegantly demonstrates that splitter cells encode the trajectory, not the destination!

https://elifesciences.org/articles/15986

6 - HD lesions & field repetition - 2017

Paper 3 showed that place fields repeat in a parallel but not radial maze.

Here we learn that disrupting the head direction signal triggers place field repetition also in the radial maze!

https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0960982217310096
